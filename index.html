Plumbing02 Jul 2026 · 2 min read

PPR vs UPVC vs CPVC vs HDPE: Choosing the Right Pipe

Four plastics, four very different jobs. A two-minute guide for plumbing and utility lines in the Kingdom.

Read article
  • PPR — hot & cold potable water inside buildings; heat-fusion joints, long service life.
  • UPVC — cold-water pressure lines, drainage & sewage; economical and chemical-resistant.
  • CPVC — hot water and select chemical lines where fusion welding is not practical; solvent-cement joints.
  • HDPE — buried water mains, irrigation & ducting; flexible coils with butt or electro-fusion welding.

Saudi summers punish the wrong choice — UV exposure, water temperature and routing all matter. Tell us the service, pressure and diameter and we will match pipe, fittings and jointing systems from stock. Send your line list for pricing.

PPE Compliance Checklist for Saudi Construction & Industrial Sites

A head-to-toe checklist to keep crews compliant and audits painless.

Read article
  • Head & eyes — ratchet-suspension helmets, impact-rated eyewear, face shields for grinding.
  • Respiratory — dust masks for concrete work; half or full-face respirators with the correct cartridges for coatings and chemicals.
  • Hands & feet — cut-resistant gloves for steel work, chemical gloves for solvents, steel or composite toe footwear.
  • Body & height — FR coveralls near hot work, hi-vis vests, and full-body harnesses with energy-absorbing lanyards for work at height.
  • Site systems — LOTO kits, signage, first aid and spill kits positioned before work starts.
